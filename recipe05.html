SEVICHE DE SOJA

SOYBEAN SEVICHE

Representation of tipical Soybean Seviche

INGREDIENTS

Soybean

300 g

Avocado

1 piece

Tomato

5 pcs

salt

Onion

1 piece

Bay leaves

3 pcs

Cloves garlic

4 pcs

Lemon juice

Green Chili

2 pcs

PREPARATION

(1) In a pot, bring water to a boil and add salt, bay leaf, broth and soy beans. Let it boil for 15 to 20 minutes.

(2) Remove excess water from the soybeans and set aside.

(3) Finely chop all the ingredients and add them to the soy.

(4) Mix well and serve.

RETURN MAIN MENU
